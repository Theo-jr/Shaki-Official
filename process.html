Our Process

Discover the artistry and craft behind every Shaki creation

The Art of Hand Stitching

At Shaki, we believe that true luxury lies in the details. Each piece is carefully crafted using traditional hand-stitching techniques combined with modern design sensibilities.

Our process honors the rich heritage of textile craftsmanship while creating contemporary pieces that stand out for their uniqueness and quality.

From Concept to Creation

01

Design & Inspiration

Our creative journey begins with gathering inspiration from nature, architecture, and cultural elements. Our designers sketch concepts that blend traditional and contemporary aesthetics.

02

Fabric Selection

We carefully select premium natural fabrics that are both beautiful and sustainable. Each fabric is chosen for its quality, texture, and how it will complement our hand-stitching techniques.

03

Pattern Making

Our skilled pattern makers transform designs into templates, considering how the stitching will enhance the silhouette of each garment. We create patterns that flatter diverse body types.

04

Cutting & Preparation

Each fabric piece is precisely cut by hand to ensure accuracy. The pieces are then prepared for the intricate hand-stitching process that gives Shaki clothing its distinctive character.

05

Hand Stitching

The heart of our process. Our skilled artisans apply various hand-stitching techniques to create unique patterns and textures. This meticulous process can take several days for a single garment.

06

Quality Control

Each finished piece undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure perfect stitching, proper fit, and overall excellence. Only garments that meet our high standards become part of the Shaki collection.
