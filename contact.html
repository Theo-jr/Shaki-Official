Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes, we offer custom designs tailored to your specific preferences. Please contact us with your requirements for a quote.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship worldwide. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within Slovakia and 7-14 days internationally.

Can I return or exchange items?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn with tags attached. Custom orders are non-refundable.

How do I care for my Shaki garments?

Most of our items require gentle hand washing with cold water and air drying. Specific care instructions are included with each item.
